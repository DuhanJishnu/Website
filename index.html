CARBON FOOTPRINTS

A carbon footprint refers to the total amount of greenhouse gases, primarily carbon dioxide, emitted into the atmosphere as a result of human activities. These activities can include driving a car, using electricity, manufacturing products, and more. Carbon footprints contribute to global warming and climate change.

GREEN ERASER

The concept of the GREEN ERASER implies the removal or reduction of something. In this case, it's about reducing or offsetting carbon emissions. We are here to help you "ERASE" your carbon footprints or minimize them.
